TRAINERS

NICKY HOLENDER - FOUNDER & MASTER COACH

Nicky Holender is an ex-professional soccer player who played in both the English and Norwegian pro leagues before coming to the US. Nicky devised SPI with the intention of helping players improve every aspect of their performance on the football pitch. Everything has been designed from technical, tactical, physical, and mental facets of soccer to allow players to attain their maximum performance possible. Nicky has invented a revolutionary system to help a player work on his or her individual performance while progressing through a season. Having trained his coaching team in this unique method, SPI is able to offer this new approach to all its players with amazing results.

DAVID PARK - EXECUTIVE OPERATIONS OFFICER & HEAD TRAINER

David Park has stepped through all levels of youth and college soccer to reach professional in the US while achieving academic prestige. Now, under the guidance of Nicky Holender, he has become an amazing performance coach. Passionate and detail-oriented, David runs sessions with direction and purpose, and has nurtured many young talent to climb the ladder towards pro status and beyond. He is also the head of SPI Orange County and South Korea.

BENJAMIN HEITMANN - EXPERT TRAINER

Benjamin Heitmann hails from Norway and played at a high youth level in Norwegian soccer. He has studied under Nicky Holender and is our youngest head coach. Benjamin has ambition and drive, and executes sessions in a precise and engaging way that are designed to help players reach their peak.
